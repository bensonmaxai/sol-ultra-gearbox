Most AI agent workflows trust what a model says it is. Sol Ultra Gearbox verifies what actually ran.

It routes Codex work to typed Sol, Terra, and Luna roles.

Then it checks the real model, reasoning effort, sandbox, lineage, token usage, and filesystem scope from persisted runtime metadata.

If anything is missing or mismatched, it fails closed.

The built-in doctor validates six role profiles. Dry-run previews global changes, while managed apply includes rollback.

This release passed twenty-three tests and a six-role runtime smoke test. If your team needs multi-agent workflows you can audit, reproduce, and reverse, try Sol Ultra Gearbox on GitHub.
