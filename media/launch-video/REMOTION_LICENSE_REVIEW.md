# Remotion license review

- Reviewed: 2026-07-14
- Scope: `media/launch-video/`
- Installed Remotion version: 4.0.489
- Owner attestation: the organization or team using Remotion has 3 people or fewer.

## Decision

The current Remotion Free License permits commercial use by an individual or
an organization or team with 3 people or fewer. Based on the owner attestation
above, this package declares Remotion's public `free-license` key in
`remotion.config.ts`.

Official sources reviewed:

- https://github.com/remotion-dev/remotion/blob/main/LICENSE.md
- https://www.remotion.pro/license
- https://www.remotion.pro/terms

## Re-review triggers

Re-check the current Remotion terms before the next release when any of these
conditions changes:

- the organization or team using Remotion grows to 4 people or more;
- the installed Remotion major version changes;
- Remotion changes its license or commercial-use terms.

This file records the engineering compliance decision made from the current
published terms and the owner's headcount attestation. It is not legal advice.
